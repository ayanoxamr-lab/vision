AMR Smart Chain is built with state-of-the-art, quantum-resistant security at its core. All assets, transactions, and communications on the network are protected using Dilithium-based cryptography. The network also leverages onion routing and a decentralized relay architecture, making communications fully encrypted, extremely difficult to trace, and resistant to censorship. Our multi-layered security model continuously evolves through proactive updates and advanced monitoring to detect and mitigate potential threats. Thanks to the decentralized architecture, user data and assets remain fully under their control while ensuring the highest levels of privacy. At the same time, AMR Smart Chain complies with global laws and regulations. We actively combat terrorism financing and money laundering through robust AML/CFT programs and cooperate with legal authorities when required. We do this in a way that respects user privacy and aligns with the principles of decentralization—utilizing privacy-preserving compliance technologies such as selective disclosure, zero-knowledge proofs, and compliance mechanisms designed to minimize reliance on personal data. By combining advanced quantum security, true privacy, practical decentralization, and effective legal compliance, AMR Smart Chain provides a secure, flexible, and globally compliant environment for individuals, businesses, and digital communities.
